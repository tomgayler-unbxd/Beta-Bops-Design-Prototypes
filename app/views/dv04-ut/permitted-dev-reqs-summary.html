  2. The house is used as a family home

  11. Is there a road or path on that side of the house? No

Based on the evidence given by applicant, the planning officer recommended that permitted development should be refused.

The officer has submitted this comment to the applicant:

The proposal does not comply with the limitations and conditions set out in Class A of the Order, by Schedule 2, Article 3, Part 1 (Development Within the Curtilage of a Dwellinghouse) as the extension will project more than 3 metres from the main elevation, and as such it is recommended that the certificate for proposed development be REFUSED.
